# Subscribe to Assistant Bob

Assistant Bob is a personal automation system operated by Rob Cannon. Subscription
is restricted to the operator; the service does not solicit or accept third-party
subscribers.

## Subscription status

<div style="max-width:32rem;border:1px solid #ddd;padding:1rem;border-radius:6px;background:#f7f7f7;">
  <strong>Closed — operator only.</strong><br/>
  This service is operated and used solely by Rob Cannon. The operator has
  consented to receive messages by configuring their own mobile number in the
  system. Subscription is not open to additional users; there is no public
  sign-up form.
</div>

## Consent and disclosures

The operator has consented to receive SMS messages from Assistant Bob, including:

- Scheduled daily and weekly summaries
- Product back-in-stock and feed-update alerts
- Responses to messages you send to the service

**Message frequency varies** depending on activity and configured schedules,
typically 1–10 messages per day.

**Message and data rates may apply** based on your mobile carrier plan. Assistant
Bob does not charge for the service itself; carrier rates are between you and your
carrier.

## How to stop or get help

- **Stop messages:** Reply **STOP** to any message at any time. You will receive
  a single confirmation and no further messages.
- **Resubscribe:** Reply **START** to resume.
- **Help:** Reply **HELP** for instructions and contact information.
